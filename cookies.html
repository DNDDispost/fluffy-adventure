Cookie Policy

Last updated:

This Cookie Policy explains how Havern Bot ("we," "us," or "our") uses cookies and similar technologies when you access and use our website and services.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device (computer, tablet, or mobile) when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently, provide a better user experience, and to provide information to the owners of the site.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

We use the following types of cookies on our website:

Type Purpose Duration
Essential Cookies These cookies are necessary for the website to function properly. They enable core functionality such as security, network management, and account access. You cannot disable these cookies through our system. Session / Persistent
Preference Cookies These cookies enable us to remember your preferences and settings to enhance your experience with our website (e.g., your preferred language or region). 1 year
Analytics Cookies These cookies help us understand how visitors interact with our website by collecting and reporting information anonymously. This helps us improve our website structure and content. 2 years
Authentication Cookies These cookies help us recognize you when you log in to our services and remember your authentication status. Session / 30 days for "Remember Me"

3. Third-Party Cookies

In addition to our own cookies, we may also use various third-party cookies to report usage statistics of the Service and deliver relevant advertisements to you. These third parties may include:

  • Google Analytics (for website analytics)
  • Discord (for authentication purposes)
  • Payment processors (for premium subscription transactions)

4. How to Manage Cookies

Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings preferences. Here's how you can manage cookies in popular browsers:

  • Google Chrome: Settings → Privacy and security → Cookies and other site data
  • Mozilla Firefox: Options → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy
  • Microsoft Edge: Settings → Site permissions → Cookies and site data

Please note that limiting or disabling cookies may affect your experience on our website as some features may not function properly.

5. Cookie Consent

When you first visit our website, you will be shown a cookie banner requesting your consent to set cookies. By clicking "Accept All Cookies," you consent to our use of cookies as described in this Cookie Policy. If you do not wish to consent to our use of cookies, you can click "Essential Cookies Only" or adjust your browser settings to manage cookies.

6. Do Not Track

Some browsers incorporate a "Do Not Track" (DNT) feature that signals to websites you visit that you do not want to have your online activity tracked. Given that there is not yet a common understanding of how to interpret the DNT signal, we currently do not respond to DNT signals on our website.

7. Local Storage and Similar Technologies

In addition to cookies, we may also use other similar technologies to store and retrieve data on your device, such as:

  • Local Storage: Enables websites to store data with no expiration date
  • Session Storage: Similar to local storage but data is cleared when the browsing session ends
  • Web Beacons: Small transparent image files used to track user behavior and server processes

These technologies function similarly to cookies but are not stored in your browser. You can clear local storage through your browser settings.

8. Updates to This Cookie Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time in response to changing legal, technical, or business developments. When we update our Cookie Policy, we will take appropriate measures to inform you, consistent with the significance of the changes we make. We will obtain your consent to any material Cookie Policy changes if and where this is required by applicable data protection laws.
